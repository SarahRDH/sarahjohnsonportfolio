Bio

I'm Sarah Johnson, a front end web developer in Los Angeles. When the planet shut down in 2020, I could no longer work as a dental hygienist, myofunctional therapist, and continuing education lecturer. I've tinkered with markup languages off and on for years, maintaining three websites for my corporation and a non-profit. Thanks to the pandemic, I had time to dive into CS and web development and turn my hobby into a career. I love the challenge of meticulously recreating a designer's ideas into functional, interactive, and responsive sites. Though I would happily code on my mac 24/7, I do take time to hike, paddleboard, bike along the beach, bake sweets, and play Butter Royale. I'm a dog lover with a knack for details and a penchant for craft cocktails and good food.
